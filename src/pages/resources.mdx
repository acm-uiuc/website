## CS Cares

CS CARES is a confidential resource for students to discuss concerns about potential Code of Conduct violations; feel free to ask them for guidance or support in addressing any issues inside and outside of ACM. You can find more information at https://siebelschool.illinois.edu/about/cs-cares.

## Feedback
