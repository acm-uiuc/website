## ACM Paid Member Guide

If you're here to learn how to pay for membership and access all the benefits, you're in the right place! ACM Room swipe access, resume book, free printing, and discounted events are all at your fingertips!

_You do **not** need to pay to participate in all of our SIGs, committees, and most of our social events._

### Become a member

Click [here](/membership) to pay for your membership online. Only $30 for a lifetime ACM membership → $0 per day! You'll need to pay with a credit card, debit card, or bank account.
